About Us

History

Theta Chi Fraternity was founded on April 10, 1856 by Frederick Norton Freeman and Arthur Chase, military cadets at Norwich University. Stated in the original constitution is the Fraternity's purpose: To “bind by closer bonds the members to each other”, “the advancement and carrying out of any measures at the institution in which it shall be established which shall be of importance to its members,” and “the mutual benefit and improvement of all its members.”

Philanthropy

Our brothers perpetuate our motto, "An Assisting Hand," as we strive to be active in our community and on our campus.
